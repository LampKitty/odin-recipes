Lasagna recipe

Description

This classic lasagna is made with an easy meat sauce as the base.
Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly!
This is great for feeding a big family, and freezes well, too.

Ingredients

  • Cheese filling
  • Tomato paste
  • Seasoning
  • Minced meat
  • Spinach

Steps

  1. Layer, cover it tightly in foil to retain the moisture.
  2. Once cooked, remove the foil, top with cheese
    and return to the oven for another 15 minutes.
  3. Serve this easy lasagna with a chunk of Homemade Garlic Bread.
