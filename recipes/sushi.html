Sushi recipe

Description

Homemade Sushi is so much cheaper than at the restaurant.
Sushi is easy and fun to make at home, and you can put
all you favourite ingredients into it

ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Rice
  • Nori seaweed
  • Cream cheese
  • Avocado
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Place the seaweed on a bamboo mat, then cover the sheet of
    seaweed with an even layer of prepared sushi rice
    Smooth gently with the rice paddle.
  2. Layer salmon, cream cheese, and avocado on the rice,
    and roll it up tightly.
    Slice with a sharp knife, and enjoy with soy sauce.
