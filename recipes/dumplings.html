Dumplings recipe

Description

My mother used to make a lot of dumplings, and
I never ate something from microwave or package,
because I already knew the taste of homemade food

ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking powder
  • White sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Stir together flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
    Cut in butter until mixture is crumbly.
    Stir in milk and mix until a batter forms that is thick enough to be
    scooped with a spoon. Allow batter to rest for 3 to 5 minutes..
  2. Drop batter by spoonfuls into boiling stew or soup.
    Cover and simmer without lifting the lid for 15 minutes. Serve.
